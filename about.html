About us

We are a studio based on students for students. It does not matter which school you belong to we are here to help. Our vision is to help our peer students with their small business and ideas to come true. We started as a group assignment for our mentor(Abhay Sharma), where we had to create a media studio. However, the members of this organization felt that they could go further. So, they created a small team where they will take jobs and use all of their member skills to make them happen. Then shortly, the team started getting more request, even from students of other organizations. To the point where we had to build more into our own identity.
